## 핵심 요약
AI is evolving from a standalone tool into an active collaborative partner in scientific research and enterprise decision-making.

---

## 본문
The AI landscape is witnessing a monumental shift in early 2026. Rather than functioning purely as an on-demand tool, AI systems are now operating as active digital partners. In enterprise settings, these systems act as digital coworkers that autonomous teams rely on for persistent workflow execution and multi-step decision-making. 

In scientific research, particularly across physics, chemistry, and biology, AI has taken on the role of a "lab assistant." These systems are not merely processing data; they are generating novel hypotheses, designing experimental protocols, and managing long-term research iterations. This transition to collaborative AI represents a fundamental change in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), emphasizing persistent context over isolated queries.
