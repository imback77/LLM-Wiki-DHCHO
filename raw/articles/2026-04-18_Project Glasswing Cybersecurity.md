## 본문
In a landmark decision for AI safety and governance, Anthropic has officially paused the public release of its frontier model, Claude Mythos 5. The model reportedly crossed a predefined "danger threshold" due to its unprecedented, autonomous ability to identify, exploit, and patch severe software vulnerabilities across complex networks.

Recognizing the dual-use nature of this capability, Anthropic, in cooperation with national cybersecurity agencies, has launched **Project Glasswing**. This initiative aims to deploy these advanced capabilities strictly within controlled, defensive Sandbox environments. The goal is to harden critical infrastructure against zero-day exploits before malicious actors can leverage similar agentic AI frameworks offensively. It marks the first time a major lab has proactively withheld a finished flagship model purely on security grounds.
