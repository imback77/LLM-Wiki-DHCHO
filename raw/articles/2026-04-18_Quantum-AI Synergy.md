## 본문
The intersection of quantum computing and artificial intelligence has yielded a major breakthrough this month. Collaborative research teams have successfully demonstrated a hybrid quantum-classical AI model capable of predicting highly chaotic systems—such as advanced fluid dynamics and micro-weather patterns—with unprecedented accuracy.

By utilizing open-source AI models to aid in quantum processor calibration and error correction, the hybrid system operates using a fraction of the memory required by traditional classical supercomputers. This synergy not only accelerators the practical utility of NISQ (Noisy Intermediate-Scale Quantum) devices but also provides new neural architectures capable of modeling non-linear, unpredictable variables that previously eluded deep learning algorithms.
